DEARMAN is an acronym that can be followed to structure a request asking for what you need and saying no assertively. This is excellent preparation to handle [[Communication#Conflict Resolution|conflict resolution]].  
  
* What did you want to ask for or say no to?  
* (D) Describe the situation that you are reacting to. Stick to the facts and use nonjudgmental statements.  
* (E) Express how you feel about the situation.  
* (A) Ask for what you want or clearly say no.  
* (R) Reinforce your position before the other person responds by describing the benefits of getting what you want.
